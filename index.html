Program Overview
Wellness Innovation
Zone Program

The NFC Wellness Innovation Zone Program is a structured, multi-phase initiative to plan, fund, build, and grow outdoor fitness infrastructure across communities. Use the roadmap on the left to navigate each phase and stage.

Phase 1

Foundation — from initial briefing through buildout & launch across 8 coordinated stages.

Phase 2

Expansion — confirm funding, sites, and timeline for the next round; activate data tracking.

Phase 3

Scale — continue expanding the program with data-informed decisions and broader reach.

Phase 1 · Stage 1
Briefing &
Masterplan
Initiation

The program kicks off with a comprehensive briefing and executive summary of the masterplan. This stage aligns stakeholders and establishes the vision, goals, and scope of the Wellness Innovation Zone Program.

📋
Executive Summary Preparation

NFC prepares a structured masterplan executive summary tailored to the city/county leadership.

🤝
Stakeholder Alignment

Key internal and external stakeholders are introduced to program goals, structure, and expectations.

Phase 1 · Stage 2
Briefing
Completed
Milestone

With the briefing formally completed, all parties have a shared understanding of the program. This milestone confirms readiness to advance to the evaluation stage.

✅
Formal Sign-Off

City/county leadership acknowledges the briefing and confirms intent to proceed.

📁
Documentation Filed

All briefing materials and executive summary are filed and shared with relevant parties.

Phase 1 · Stage 3
Evaluation
Stage
Assessment

The city/county undergoes an internal evaluation to assess fit, feasibility, and alignment with community goals. NFC supports this process with data, program documentation, and Q&A sessions.

🔍
Community Needs Assessment

Evaluate existing fitness infrastructure, gaps, and community demand.

📊
Feasibility Review

Assess budget capacity, political will, and timeline alignment.

Phase 1 · Stage 4
Application
Stage
~2 Weeks

The city/county submits a non-binding qualification application. This lightweight process lets both parties assess alignment before committing deeper resources.

Non-Binding Application

A streamlined qualification form completed in approximately 2 weeks — no obligation to continue.

NFC Review

NFC evaluates the application for program fit and prepares a preliminary recommendation.

Phase 1 · Stage 5
Resolution of
Adoption (ROA)
Formal Commitment

The governing body passes a Resolution of Adoption — a formal commitment to the program. The ROA signals institutional buy-in and unlocks access to funding pathways and deeper program resources.

🏛️
Council Resolution

City council or county board formally adopts the resolution and enters it into the official record.

📜
Executed Agreement

Signed documentation exchanged between the municipality and NFC, formalizing the partnership.

Phase 1 · Stage 6
Fundraising
Stage
Parallel Workstreams

Three parallel workstreams drive the fundraising stage: identifying funding avenues, securing sponsors, and locking in sites. NFC actively supports all three tracks.

💰 Funding Avenues

Identify fiscal agents, foundations, and funding paths. Prioritize existing relationships and known alignment partners.

🤝 Sponsorship

Target sponsors, prioritize city/county introductions. NFC creates sponsor portals and holds briefings.

📍 Sites

Confirm with planning team, achieve council consensus, engage community groups, review existing infrastructure, and conduct NFC on-the-ground site visits.

P1

Phase 1 Site Identification

Identify specific sites, configurations, and overall masterplan (number of sites) for Phase 1 deployment.

Phase 1 · Stage 7
Funding
Confirmed
Gate Milestone

All three funding and readiness gates must be confirmed before advancing to buildout. This stage is a critical checkpoint ensuring financial, political, and physical readiness.

🏛️
Council & City Match Confirmed

Municipal matching funds and council authorization formally confirmed.

🏢
Sponsors Confirmed

Sponsor commitments finalized with signed agreements or letters of intent.

📍
Sites Confirmed

All Phase 1 site locations formally approved by planning, council, and community stakeholders.

Phase 1 · Stage 8
Buildout &
Launch
Execution

The program becomes physical reality. Equipment ships, installation teams mobilize, and the community celebrates with a press & media ribbon cutting event.

🚚 Shipment

Fitness equipment is manufactured and shipped to confirmed site locations on agreed timelines.

🔧 Installation

NFC-approved installation teams complete equipment setup at each Phase 1 site.

🎉 Ribbon Cutting

Press, media, and community stakeholders gather for a high-profile launch event celebrating the program's arrival.

Phase 2:

Building on Success

Phase 2 expands the program to additional sites. Lessons learned from Phase 1 inform site selection and fundraising strategy. Data tracking begins in earnest during this phase.

Confirm Funding

Secure financial commitments for Phase 2 expansion sites.

Confirm Sites

Launch Timeline

Phase 3 Overview
Phase 3:
Scale
Data-Driven Growth

Phase 3 leverages accumulated data and momentum to scale the program further. Site selection, funding strategies, and community engagement are all refined by real-world results from Phases 1 and 2.

Confirm Funding

Secure financial commitments using Phase 2 impact data to attract new sponsors.

Confirm Sites

Data-informed site selection for maximum community impact.

Continued Data

Ongoing tracking builds a multi-year dataset for reporting and advocacy.

Phase 3 · Step 2

Sites

Phase 3 · Step 4

📈

📣
Impact Reporting
